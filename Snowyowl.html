The Snowy Owl


Summary from Wikipedia on Snowy Owls

  • Size: Very large
    • One of the largest owls in the world
    • About the sixth or seventh heaviest living owl
  • Habitat: The northern circumpolar region
    • Native to the Arctic regions of both North America and the Palearctic
  • Vulnerable
  • Nocturnal
  • Carvivores
    • Other birds, such as Willow ptarmigan and Rock ptarmigan
    • Eat Lemmings -- A species of rodent
